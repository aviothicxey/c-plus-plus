Recipe

This is a delicious recipe for a biscuit cake.Hope you will like it .

Total time to cook : 45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter

Preparations

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

    Time: 10 minutes

  2. Mix flour, sugar, and cocoa powder in a bowl.

    Time: 5 minutes

  3. Add eggs, milk, and butter; mix until smooth.

    Time: 10 minutes

  4. Pour batter into a greased pan and bake.

    Time: 40 minutes

  5. Let the cake cool before serving.

    Time: 25 minutes
